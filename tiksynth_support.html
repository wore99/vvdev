Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I start using TikSynth?

Download the app from the Mac App Store and follow the simple setup instructions. After installation, you can start creating your first project by clicking the "New Project" button.

2. What are the system requirements?

TikSynth requires macOS 12.0 or later. We recommend at least 8GB RAM and 2GB free disk space for optimal performance.

3. How do I report a bug?

Please email us at vanny_yu@qq.com with the following information:

  • Your macOS version
  • TikSynth version
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
