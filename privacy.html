Privacy Policy for Breathe & Nap

Last updated: September 7, 2024

Introduction

Breathe & Nap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Breathe & Nap application.

Information We Don't Collect

Breathe & Nap is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data about your usage of the app. All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  1. Nap duration preferences
  2. Alarm settings for nap mode
  3. Breathing exercise duration preferences
  4. Breathing mode selection (4-7-8 or 4-4-4-4)

This information is used solely to provide you with a personalized experience and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Audio Files

The app includes pre-loaded audio files for breathing exercises and alarms. These files are part of the app installation and do not collect or transmit any data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
