Sunrise Yoga – help and FAQs

Getting Started

  • Pick a sequence and tap the play button to begin.
  • Follow the on‑screen pose images and the spoken cues.
  • The round counter shows your progress (e.g., 2/5).

Audio Guidance

  • Check that your device is not in silent mode and volume is turned up.
  • Audio plays through the system speaker or connected Bluetooth devices.

Troubleshooting

  • If audio doesn’t play, restart the app and try again.
  • If the screen dims, keep the app open during practice.

Contact

Need help or want to share feedback? Email us at support@sunriseyoga.app.
