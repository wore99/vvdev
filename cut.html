Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Re-record feature work?

Simply select the segment (sentence) you want to fix in the list, click the "Re-record" button, and record your new take. The app will automatically replace the old segment with your new recording in the timeline.

Can I import my own scripts?

Yes! You can copy and paste any text into the script editor. The teleprompter will automatically adjust to your text.

Where are my videos saved?

Your projects are saved locally on your Mac. When you export a video, you can choose the destination folder.
