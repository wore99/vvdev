SportsWeather Support

Last Updated: 2026-04-26

Thanks for using SportsWeather. If you run into issues with place matching, weather loading, or recommended time slots, check the guidance below.

1. FAQ

Q: What should I do if location access fails?
  • Enable location permission for this app in iPhone Settings (recommended: "While Using the App").
  • You can also tap + to manually add a city or place and continue.
Q: Why can't my city name be matched?
  • Use English city names, such as New York or Shanghai.
  • If matching is unstable, add region details such as New York, NY.
Q: Why does weather data look inaccurate?
  • The app may use different weather providers, so update timing can differ from other platforms.
  • Try refreshing later or retrying on another network.

2. Contact Support

For support, email us at: support@sportsweather.app

Please include the information below to help us troubleshoot faster:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Time and city where the issue occurred
  • Screenshots (if available)

3. Response Time

We usually reply within 3-5 business days. Response may take longer during holidays.
