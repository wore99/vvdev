Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 Feb 2026

Sunrise Yoga respects your privacy. We design the app to run fully on‑device and to collect no personal data.

What We Collect

Nothing. Sunrise Yoga:

  • Does not require an account or sign‑in.
  • Does not collect personal or usage data.
  • Does not use third‑party analytics or ad SDKs.
  • Does not track you across apps or websites.

On‑Device Speech

The voice guidance uses Apple's on‑device speech synthesis. Voice prompts are generated locally; no audio is uploaded to our servers (we do not operate servers for this app).

Permissions

  • Audio Output: Needed to play spoken guidance. No recording or microphone access is used.

Children’s Privacy

Sunrise Yoga is appropriate for all ages and collects no personal data.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the “Last updated” date. You can review this policy at any time.

Contact

If you have questions, please contact us at support@sunriseyoga.app.
